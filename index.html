About Me

I am Sara, your fellow bootcamper from Birmingham. Like most of you I have joined this incredible bootcamp to gain technical skills and break into the fascinating world of tech!

I studied English at university as I was a bit of a book-worm and loved all things literature and analysing. I initially wanted to be a teacher but did a complete turn and decided tech is where I wanted to be!. I have worked as a tutor and business administrator.

I am a mother of two and enjoy travelling and creating memories with my children. I have lived in a few countries like Denmark and Qatar and love exploring new cultures. If am not spending time with my kids, I enjoy going on walks and reading. I am a big fan of photography, although I am pretty useless at taking good photos I like to admire the great moments captured by others.
